This is a collection of conceptual art and design work by Boston-based artist Dave Cohn. Most of the projects here focus on topics in deconstruction. The heading, "Name-Games," is meant to draw attention to the unity of these projects in their specific effort against totality. These artworks give way to a freer mode of signification by means of their partialness: they allude to inherent ambiguities in the realms of identity and language so as to clear a space that is empty of words by retreating out of it.
