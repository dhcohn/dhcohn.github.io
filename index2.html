This is a collection of conceptual art and design work by Boston-based artist Dave Cohn. Most of the projects here focus on topics in deconstruction. They are "Name-games" because they invite an endless exchange of proper nouns: rather than staking claims, these projects prefer to vacate a space and secure the vacancy.
