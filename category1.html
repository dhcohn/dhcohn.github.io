These music experiments began with a USB compressor microphone, an old laptop, a crappy drumset and software synths from garageband. My process of recording drum phrases and looping and chopping them evolved as I began to incorporate a better microphone set-up and more sophisticated software. The results usually express a single musical thought while the constant variation simulates spatial distance. Inspired by the philosophy of Henri Bergson.
